# FAQ

#### I have a question about the project, where can I ask it?
You can ask your question in the [Discussions](https://github.com/ZingerLittleBee/server_bee-backend/discussions) section of this repository.

#### I found a bug, where can I report it?
You can report a bug in the [Issues](https://github.com/ZingerLittleBee/server_bee-backend/issues/new) section of this repository.

#### I want to contribute to the project, how can I do that?
You can contribute to the project by forking the repository and creating a pull request. You can also contribute by reporting bugs and suggesting new features.

#### I want to suggest a new feature, where can I do that?
You can suggest a new feature in the [Issues](https://github.com/ZingerLittleBee/server_bee-backend/issues/new) section of this repository.

#### I want to contact the project owner, how can I do that?
You can contact the project owner by sending an email to support@serverbee.app

#### I want to support the project, how can I do that?
You can support the project by starring the repository and sharing it with others.

#### I want to donate to the project, how can I do that?
You can purchase a **ServerBee** subscription to the project on the [AppStore](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/serverbee/id6443553714).
